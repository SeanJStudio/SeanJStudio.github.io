Claymates

I like crypto, art and gamification, so I made a gamified NFT collection called Claymates!

Claymates is an NFT collection showcasing 666 clay themed characters I created. The first 198 tokens start as a chest themed to 1 of 21 kingdoms. Unlock it reveal your mate and then breed a pair for a small fee to get a new one!

Built on Hedera and fully on-chain this collection will live forever (or until Hederas network shuts down). Tech used: HieroSDK, HGraph Standards SDK, Node, Vite, HTML, CSS, Vercel, Open AI Sora, Google Veo3.
