Papa Johns Pizza

Papa Johns wanted to do a direct mail campaign in BC and needed my help. To show them how to get the most coverage, I made a Tableau dashboard comparing the amount of people who have opted-out of admail vs opted-in (consumers choice) by FSA (forward sortation area). This allowed them to do neighborhood mail in low opt-out areas and postal code targeting mail in high opt out areas.

Try out the dashboard by hovering your cursor around the map or clicking the grey band at the top to see where the most-opt outs are. Please note I've disabled panning and zooming to maintain the usability of my website.
