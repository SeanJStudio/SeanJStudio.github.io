Papa Johns Pizza

I had the opportunity to work with Papa Johns on a direct mail campaign in BC. I was tasked with creating a Tableau dashboard that would provide insights into the number of consumers who opted-out of admail versus those who opted-in, categorized by FSA (forward sortation area). My objective was to help Papa Johns achieve maximum coverage while ensuring that the campaign was well-received by their target audience.

I developed a user-friendly Tableau dashboard that allowed Papa Johns to conduct a neighborhood mail campaign in low opt-out areas and a targeted postal code mail campaign in high opt-out areas. To access the dashboard, simply hover your cursor around the map or click on the grey band at the top of the page to view the most-opted-out areas.

Please note that I have disabled the panning and zooming features of the dashboard to ensure a seamless user experience on my portfolio website.
