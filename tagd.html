Tagd

Tagd is a simple application I developed in Java using Apache PDFBox to create quality control forms for ranges of printed material. For example a printer will check the quality of a print run every 500 pieces and sign off the form and slide it into the printed material. The PDF contains 4 forms imposed on a letter sized sheet in cut and stack order to reduce paper waste and keep the forms easily in order. My favorite part is the little customer database it holds so it can auto suggest customers to users as they type.
