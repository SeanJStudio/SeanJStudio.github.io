Union Gospel Mission

I had the opportunity to work with Union Gospel Mission, a charitable organization that focuses on helping the homeless population in Metro Vancouver. My role involved analyzing their donor data and past campaigns to provide strategic direction for their direct mail marketing campaign, which was one of their largest campaigns of the year.

Through my analysis, I was able to identify the most effective types of mail for their budget, allowing them to maximize their resources and make a greater impact in the community.
