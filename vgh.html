VGH Hometown Heroes

VGH Hometown Heroes is a popular annual lottery to raise money for a great cause. They wanted to unlock insights about their donors and strategy for future direct mail campaigns. So I processed and analyzed their donor data and past campaigns for explorative insights and bridge a connection to the most effective types of mail.

I like how visually striking the colors are on this dashboard and how cool it is being able to switch views from a dropdown. Please note for privacy reasons I have not embedded this dashboard.
